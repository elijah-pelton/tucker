Our LEED Commitment

Pioneering Green Hospitality

The Meridian is the first LEED-certified business hotel in Downtown Long Beach, integrating measurable systems that drastically reduce our environmental footprint and operational costs.

Renewable Energy Focus

We utilize a powerful 30-40 kW grid-tied solar array and high-efficiency VRF systems, significantly cutting energy consumption and carbon emissions.

Measurable Water Savings

Our advanced greywater reuse system saves approximately 50 gallons of water per occupied room, redirecting water for non-potable uses.

Sourced & Recycled Materials

Sustainability is integral, from reclaimed wood used in interiors to our exclusive use of eco-certified cleaning supplies and local vendors.
